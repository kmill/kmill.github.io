1. Course Policies

Grading. 70% exams (20% for each midterm, 30% for the final), and the remainder is half homework and half quizzes. The lowest quiz grade will be dropped.

Quizzes. There will be a fifteen-minute quiz every Wednesday at the beginning of discussion. We will go over the solutions to the quiz at the following meeting.

E-mail. I will not answer e-mails that ask how to solve particular homework problems which do not give some evidence of your having tried something. The night before an exam, I may have to triage, prioritizing administrative and then quick math clarification questions over everything else.
